Random Fits Free Address Autocomplete

This version does NOT require Google Places or any paid API.

How it works:
- Customer types in the Address field.
- After 3 characters, the site searches free public address suggestions.
- It checks common typos like "guildford" -> "guilford".
- It shows matching address suggestions when available.
- It always gives a "Use typed address" option so checkout is never blocked.
- ZIP still auto-fills city/state when possible.

Important:
Free address autocomplete will not be as perfect as Shopify or Google.
It can miss exact house numbers sometimes, but it costs nothing and needs no key.
